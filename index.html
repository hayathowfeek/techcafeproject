Technology for Enhancing Second Language English Literacy Education

This site aims to serve as an information source regarding the research project titled “Technology for Enhancing Second Language English Literacy Education”. The goal of this research project was to design accessible and effective computing tools for enhancing English literacy skills among children and adults in several different regions.



We are grateful to the many individuals and organizations who supported the work presented on this website. This work, conducted from 2012-1015, was made possible by NPRP grant # 4-439-1-071 from the Qatar National Research Fund (a member of Qatar Foundation).
