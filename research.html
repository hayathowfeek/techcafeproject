Research

The aim of the project was to advance the state-of-the-art in educational technology and in ICTD (Information and Communication Technology for Development) ​by developing and field testing innovative tools and games designed to improve literacy among children and among adults in several countries including Qatar, the United States, and some developing countries.



Audience

In this work we explored effective computing tools to enhance literacy for four primary audiences throughout the duration of the project during different stages of development:

  • Adults learning English as a second or foreign language
  • Children learning English as a second or foreign language
  • Youth learning English at university level
  • English language learners with special needs

Technology

Our goal was to develop an educational gaming platform, with mobile and web based games and teacher tools, to provide English education to our target audience. Over the course of the project a number of games were created to do so.
